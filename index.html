About

Lionel Andrés Messi; born 24 June 1987, is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward for Ligue 1 club Paris Saint-Germain and captains the Argentina national team. Widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time, Messi has won a record seven Ballon d'Or awards, and a record six European Golden Shoes. He has won 37 trophies in his senior career, including eleven league titles, four UEFA Champions Leagues, and the Copa América. Messi holds the records for the most goals, most assists, and most hat-tricks in La Liga, most goals in a European league season, most assists in a La Liga season, and most hat-tricks in the UEFA Champions League. He also holds the record for most assists in the Copa América and most international goals by a South American male. Messi has scored over 785 senior career goals for club and country, and has the most goals by a player for a single club.
